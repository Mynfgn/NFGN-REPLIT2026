# NFGN DIGITAL BOOK STORE™, AUTHOR MARKETPLACE & KNOWLEDGE LIBRARY

## COMPLETE REPLIT MASTER BUILD SPECIFICATION

### PROJECT OVERVIEW

Build a complete Digital Book Store, Author Marketplace, Audiobook Library, and Knowledge Library for NFGN.

The platform should function similarly to Apple Books, Kindle, Audible, Gumroad, and a digital learning platform combined.

The system must be integrated into both:

1. NFGN Admin Back Office
2. NFGN Member Back Office

The platform must support:

* eBooks
* Audiobooks
* Digital Training Manuals
* Health & Wellness Guides
* Bible Studies
* Business Training Materials
* Leadership Training
* Personal Development Books
* NFGN Educational Resources
* Future Course Integration
* Future Video Library Integration

IMPORTANT:

Customers are purchasing a LICENSE TO READ.

Customers DO NOT receive:

* Copyright Ownership
* Resale Rights
* Reproduction Rights
* Distribution Rights
* Commercial Rights

Copyright ownership always remains with the author and/or NFGN.

# SALES & ROYALTY SYSTEM

IMPORTANT:

Admin must be able to completely modify all payout structures.

Admin Settings:

* Platform Fee %
* Author Royalty %
* Affiliate Commission %
* Referral Commission %
* Promotional Bonuses
* Category-Based Royalties
* Book-Specific Royalties
* Author-Specific Royalties

Default Example:

Platform Fee = 30%

Author Royalty = 70%

HOWEVER:

Admin can modify at any time.

Examples:

Book A:
Platform = 20%
Author = 80%

Book B:
Platform = 40%
Author = 60%

Book C:
Platform = Fixed Dollar Amount

Book D:
Custom Commission Structure

Admin Controls:

* Edit Platform Fee %

* Override Per Category
* Pause Royalties
* Bonus Royalties

# FINAL OBJECTIVE

Create a complete NFGN Digital Book Store™, Author Marketplace™, Audiobook Library™, and Knowledge Vault™ that allows:

* NFGN to sell books
* Members to purchase reading licenses
* Approved members to become authors
* Authors to earn royalties
* Admin to control and modify all commissions, royalties, fees, subscriptions, and payout structures
* Members to read books securely inside the platform
* Future expansion into courses, videos, certifications, coaching, and digital products

Build using modern scalable architecture, reusable components, secure authentication, role-based permissions, and enterprise-level design standards.
