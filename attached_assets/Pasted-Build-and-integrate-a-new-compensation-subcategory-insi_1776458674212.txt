Build and integrate a new compensation subcategory inside the NFGN MLM platform called:

PRO CONSULTANT COMMISSIONS → BILL PAYER PROGRAM (BPP)

Company Name:
New Face Global Network (NFGN)

Purpose of this feature:
Create a “Bill Payer Program” (BPP) for NFGN Pro Members only. This is a special bonus program designed to help qualified Pro Members pay important monthly bills, regardless of how many products they are personally selling by themselves, because this bonus is primarily based on Group Volume (GV) performance, not personal retail sales alone.

The concept:
A Pro Member earns a Bill Payer Bonus when they help their group/community generate enough volume and they also meet the required minimum Personal Volume (PV). These bonuses are funded by the company as part of the compensation structure and should be deposited into the member’s NFGN account wallet/balance once the qualification requirements are met and approved.

==================================================
CORE BUSINESS RULES
==================================================

1. The Bill Payer Program (BPP) must be available to PRO MEMBERS ONLY.
   - Standard members, affiliates, customers, and non-Pro consultants cannot access or qualify for BPP.
   - The system should validate Pro membership status before showing eligibility or payout qualification.

2. Add a new compensation subcategory:
   - Main Section: Pro Consultant Commissions
   - New Subcategory: Bill Payer Program (BPP)

3. The BPP contains 5 separate “Funds”:
   - Rent / Mortgage Fund
   - Car Fund
   - Utilities Fund
   - Medical Fund
   - Phone / Internet Fund

4. Default maximum payout caps for each fund:
   - Rent / Mortgage Fund: up to $1,500
   - Car Fund: up to $600
   - Utilities Fund: up to $450
   - Medical Fund: up to $350
   - Phone / Internet Fund: up to $185

5. Important qualification rule:
   - These funds CANNOT be earned by personally selling products only.
   - They are earned by helping the member’s group/community become productive and successful.
   - Qualification must be based primarily on GROUP VOLUME (GV), plus a minimum PERSONAL VOLUME (PV) requirement.
   - The platform should emphasize that the network is rewarding leadership, duplication, support, and team performance.

6. Deposit behavior:
   - Once requirements are met and approved, the Bill Payer Bonus should be deposited into the member’s NFGN account/wallet.
   - Show admin-configurable messaging that states payouts are usually deposited within 1 to 5 business days.
   - Actual payout cycle should be monthly.
   - Funds should be paid no more than once per month per fund unless admin overrides are enabled.

7. Non-transferable rule:
   - Funds cannot be transferred to another member.
   - Funds cannot be reassigned, relocated, gifted, or paid out to someone else.
   - These bonuses belong only to the qualifying Pro Member.

8. Admin disclaimer:
   - The total amount of each fund can be changed at any time at company discretion.
   - Build this as editable language in admin settings and display it in the member-facing BPP section.

9. Story / origin section:
   Add a member-facing description explaining that:
   - The origin of this fund begins with Mr. Marcelino himself, who wanted to help people struggling with bills and daily hardships.
   - He wanted to help people obtain financial freedom by taking a portion of the company’s revenue and directing it toward qualifying Pro Members.
   - The Bill Payer Program should be presented as one of the most exciting features of the NFGN platform and compensation structure.

==================================================
COMPENSATION / CALCULATION LOGIC
==================================================

Create a flexible calculation engine for the Bill Payer Program.

Each fund should support this logic:

A Pro Member qualifies for a fund if:
1. They are an active Pro Member
2. Their monthly Personal Volume (PV) is equal to or above the required PV threshold
3. Their qualifying Group Volume (GV) is equal to or above the required GV threshold
4. The fund is active
5. They have not already been paid that fund for the current monthly cycle
6. Any compliance/account standing requirements are satisfied

Suggested payout calculation:
- payout_amount = min( GV × payout_percentage, fund_max_cap )

Example:
- GV = 15,000
- Payout % = 12%
- Max cap = $1,500
- Raw amount = $1,800
- Actual payout = $1,500 cap

Another example:
- GV = 3,000
- Payout % = 12%
- Raw amount = $360
- If the user meets the threshold requirement and cap is higher than $360, payout = $360

IMPORTANT:
Make this logic configurable so the system can support either:
A. Threshold + percentage payout
or
B. Flat payout when threshold is met

Add an admin option for payout mode per fund:
- Percentage-Based
- Flat Amount

If Flat Amount is selected:
- Pay the preset fund amount once the required GV and PV are met
- Example:
  Rent Fund = fixed $1,500 if qualified

If Percentage-Based is selected:
- Pay percentage of GV, limited by the fund cap
