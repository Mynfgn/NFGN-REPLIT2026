NFGN Pay As You Go (PAYG)
What Is NFGN's Pay As You Go Program?
NFGN's Pay As You Go (PAYG) Program is a unique booking and referral platform that operates within the NFGN Book-A-Professional system. PAYG was designed to connect customers with qualified service providers while creating additional income opportunities for professionals, business owners, and referring members.
Unlike traditional service directories, PAYG combines professional service bookings, product sales, referral commissions, and business development into one system. The goal is simple: help professionals increase their income, help customers find trusted service providers, and create additional earning opportunities throughout the NFGN network.
How It Started
The development of the Pay As You Go Program began in November 2014 by Joe Marcelino, Founder of New Face Global Network (NFGN).
At the time, NFGN was marketing and selling hair extensions and beauty-related products. Joe noticed that many cosmetologists and hairstylists were purchasing hair bundles and products through the company, but there was no organized system to connect those professionals with customers who needed installation and beauty services.
His vision was straightforward:
If a customer purchased hair extensions from NFGN, why not refer that customer to an NFGN-affiliated cosmetologist who could install the hair?
This approach would create benefits for everyone involved:
The customer receives professional service.
The cosmetologist gains a new client.
The cosmetologist sells more products.
The referring member earns referral commissions.
NFGN increases overall product movement and customer satisfaction.
What began as a simple referral concept quickly evolved into a much larger business model.

Why PAYG Is So Powerful For Salon Owners
One of the biggest challenges salon owners face is managing traditional booth rentals.
Many salon owners charge weekly or monthly rental fees and often deal with:
Missed payments
Vacant chairs
Unused space
Long-term commitments
Administrative headaches
PAYG offers a different solution.
Instead of relying solely on monthly booth rental agreements, salon owners can offer flexible hourly rentals through the Pay As You Go system.
This allows salon owners to:
Monetize unused chairs
Increase occupancy rates
Generate revenue from empty workstations
Reduce financial risk
Create flexible opportunities for stylists
A chair that sits empty generates no revenue. A chair rented through PAYG creates income immediately.
Helping Professionals Reduce Overhead
Another major advantage of PAYG is that it helps professionals increase their earning potential without the burden of excessive overhead expenses.
For example, a cosmetologist may not be ready to commit to a full-time booth rental. Through PAYG, they can rent space only when needed, serve their clients, and keep more of their profits.
This flexibility allows professionals to:
Grow their clientele
Build their brand
Increase profitability
Minimize unnecessary expenses
Scale at their own pace
Creating Multiple Streams of Income
Perhaps the most exciting aspect of PAYG is its ability to create multiple streams of income from a single customer relationship.
For example:
A cosmetologist performs a hairstyle service for $50.
Traditionally, the transaction ends there.
Within the NFGN ecosystem, however, that same customer may also purchase:
Handmade soaps
Handmade candles
Herbal wellness products
Weight-loss programs
Health and wellness consultations
Travel services
Educational courses
Memberships
Special events
Additional professional services
As a result, the cosmetologist is no longer limited to earning money only when a customer sits in their chair.
They can participate in a larger ecosystem that allows customers to continue purchasing products and services throughout the week, month, and year.
This creates the opportunity for long-term residual income and ongoing customer relationships.
The Future of PAYG
Today, Pay As You Go continues to evolve as one of the most innovative components of the NFGN platform.
Its purpose remains the same as it was in 2014:
To connect customers with professionals, help business owners generate additional revenue, increase product movement throughout the network, reward referrals, and create opportunities for individuals to build stronger and more sustainable incomes.
PAYG is more than a booking system. It is a business-building platform designed to help professionals, entrepreneurs, and service providers expand their reach, increase their earnings, and participate in a thriving network of products, services, and opportunities.
