Create and optimize the BOOK-A-PROFESSIONAL section inside the NFGN Admin and Member Back Office.
MAIN MENU STRUCTURE
Under the main menu category BOOK-A-PROFESSIONAL, create the following 4 submenu sections:
Providers
Bookings
Pay-As-You-Go
Earnings
BOOKINGS SECTION
The Bookings section already exists. Keep it under BOOK-A-PROFESSIONAL.
All BOOK-A-PROFESSIONAL bookings/orders must appear in Bookings once they are:
Paid for
Processed
Rendered/completed
Pending approval
Approved
Denied
Refunded
Cancelled
PAY-AS-YOU-GO SECTION
Under Pay-As-You-Go, create a dropdown menu with:
PAYG Approvals
PAYG Bookings
Move “Pay As You Go Provider Approvals” under Pay-As-You-Go and rename it to:
PAYG Approvals
PAYG Bookings must also be connected to the main Bookings section, so PAYG bookings can be viewed both under:
BOOK-A-PROFESSIONAL > Pay-As-You-Go > PAYG Bookings
and
BOOK-A-PROFESSIONAL > Bookings
PROVIDERS SECTION
Under Providers, create 9 main provider categories:
Health and Wellness
Cosmetology
Restaurants
Professional Services
Consultant Services
Education
Marketing and Advertisement
General Services
NFGN Sports
Each provider category must include:
Category title
Professional description
Purpose of the category
Who the category is for
List of providers assigned to that category
Admin input/add/edit/delete provider capability
The Admin must be able to add a provider directly under the correct category.
Example:
If the Admin wants to add a hairstylist, barber, nail tech, massage therapist, or spa provider, the Admin should be able to add that provider directly under Cosmetology.
PROVIDER INPUT FIELDS
When the Admin adds a provider, include these fields:

Price or price range
Pay-As-You-Go eligibility
Commissionable status
Commission amount or percentage
Location
Phone number
Email address
Website or booking link
Upload image/logo
Provider status: Pending, Approved, Denied, Suspended
Notes
Created date
Updated date
PROVIDER CATEGORY DESCRIPTIONS
Health and Wellness:
For providers offering wellness, healthcare, fitness, nutrition, mental health, naturopathic, primary care, and personal wellness services.
Cosmetology:
For beauty, grooming, spa, hair, nails, skincare, massage, and personal care providers.
Restaurants:
For restaurants, food trucks, meal services, catering, and food-related vendors.
Professional Services:
For skilled trades, repair services, event locations, business services, and professional service providers.
Consultant Services:
For business coaches, MLM consultants, spiritual consultants, life coaches, counselors, and professional advisors.
Education:
For tutors, instructors, trainers, educational coaches, and learning support providers.
Marketing and Advertisement:
For marketing specialists, branding experts, advertising providers, content creators, and promotional service providers.
General Services:
For everyday service providers such as ride share, lawn care, home services, mechanics, painters, carpenters, and other local service providers.
NFGN Sports:
For sports coaches, trainers, teams, camps, player development, private lessons, and athletic services.

EARNINGS SECTION
Under BOOK-A-PROFESSIONAL, create an Earnings section.
Any money earned from completed bookings must show under:
BOOK-A-PROFESSIONAL > Earnings
Admin > Commissions
Member Back Office > Earnings
Member Back Office > Commissions
Only completed services should count as earned income.
A completed service means:
Customer paid
Booking was confirmed
Service was rendered/provided
Booking status changed to Completed
Commission was calculated
Earnings must include:
Booking ID
Customer name
Provider name
Service category
Service subcategory
Service date
Payment amount
Commission amount
Admin earnings
Provider earnings
Member earnings
Status
Payment date
Completion date

COMMISSION LOGIC
When a booking is marked Completed, calculate and display the appropriate earnings/commissions in:
BOOK-A-PROFESSIONAL Earnings

Do not count unpaid, cancelled, denied, or refunded bookings as earned income.
