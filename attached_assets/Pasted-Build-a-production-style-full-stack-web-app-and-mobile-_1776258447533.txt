CORE BUSINESS MODEL
This platform uses a “2 Down By Infinity Pay Structure” with 9 generations of payout tracking.

REQUIRED MLM RULES
1. A referring consultant gets a replicated affiliate link.
2. When a customer clicks that affiliate link, the first thing they see is a beautiful NFGN replicated website / capture page showing:
   - Referring consultant’s name
   - Consultant photo/avatar
   - Consultant contact button
   - Product highlights
   - Join / Shop / Book buttons
3. The system must track the sponsor / referrer automatically through URL parameters and cookies/session.
4. Customers can buy products as retail customers.
5. Only customers who purchase a “Pro Member Registration Package” become commission-qualified.
6. Sales commissions can only be earned by qualified Pro Members.
7. Members earn 9 generations of payout.
8. Level 2 must pay double the sales commission of level 1.
