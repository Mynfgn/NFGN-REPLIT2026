Pay As You Go Service Description
Pay As You Go was originally designed for salon owners renting out chairs to hair stylists and barbers, also known as booth rentals, who need a professional location to perform their services.
For example, if a salon owner has an available chair, they can rent that chair by the hour with a 2-hour minimum requirement.
Pricing example:
First 2 hours: $5.75 per hour
Every additional hour that same day: $4.75 per hour
Maximum rental per stylist per day: 8 hours
One 8-hour rental period is called 1 Block of Time
2 Blocks of Time = 16 hours
A stylist can only purchase 1 Block of Time per day
The first 2 hours will always be charged at $5.75 each
Additional hours after the first 2 hours are charged at $4.75 each
If more than 8 hours are needed, additional hours must be requested or purchased at the time of purchase, subject to approval
Booking Rules
The customer must be able to:
Select a provider/location
Select an available service
Pick an available appointment date
Pick an available start time
Choose the number of rental hours
View total price before checkout
Purchase and confirm the booking online
Booking must be made at least 48 hours in advance.
Same-day bookings are not allowed without corporate/admin approval.
Once paid, the purchase is non-refundable.
Display this notice clearly before checkout:
“All Pay As You Go bookings must be purchased at least 48 hours in advance for scheduling and payment processing purposes. Same-day bookings require corporate approval. Once paid, all purchases are non-refundable.”

Do not place availability controls only inside the admin panel. Providers must have a dedicated area inside their own back office/dashboard where they can update their available dates and times.

Provider Services

Each service must include:

CV should be calculated automatically using:
CV = Service Price x 0.10

$100 service x 0.10 = 10 CV
$50 service x 0.10 = 5 CV
$40 service x 0.10 = 4 CV
