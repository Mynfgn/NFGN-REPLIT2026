Update the MYNFGN.COM / NFGN Platform.
Pay As You Go Provider Eligibility Requirements
Add a mandatory provider verification and approval process before any Pay As You Go services can be published or offered on the NFGN platform.
Provider Eligibility Rule
ONLY approved Pro Member Business Accounts may offer Pay As You Go services.
Examples include, but are not limited to:
Hair Salons
Barber Shops
Nail Salons
Day Spas
Massage Therapy Businesses
Wellness Centers
Fitness Studios
Dance Studios
Tutoring Centers
Photography Studios
Event Venues
Professional Service Providers
Other approved service-based businesses
Free Member accounts are NOT eligible to offer Pay As You Go services.
If a Free Member attempts to create a Pay As You Go service, display a notification encouraging them to upgrade to a Pro Membership.
Important Provider Alert
At the very beginning of the Pay As You Go setup process, display the following alert:
IMPORTANT NOTICE
Before your Pay As You Go services can be listed on the NFGN platform, your business must successfully complete the NFGN Provider Verification and Approval Process.
Only verified and approved Pro Member business accounts are permitted to offer Pay As You Go services.
Your business must have a legitimate operating business location before services can be made available to the public.
Business locations may include:
Commercial locations
Professional office locations
Salon suites
Shared workspaces
Home-based businesses (where legally permitted)
Mobile service businesses (subject to approval)
Approval is required before any service listings can go live.
Approval Time Notice
Display this warning in a highlighted notification box:
APPROVAL REQUIRED
Provider approval can take up to fourteen (14) business days.
Services will remain in "Pending Approval" status until reviewed and approved by NFGN.
Providers may not accept bookings, payments, appointments, or reservations through the NFGN platform until approval has been granted.

Fraud Prevention Notice
Display the following legal notice prominently during setup:
LEGAL NOTICE
All information submitted to NFGN must be truthful, accurate, and complete.
By submitting information through this platform, you certify that all information provided is true and correct to the best of your knowledge.
Providing false, misleading, fraudulent, stolen, or fabricated information may result in:
Immediate denial of approval
Removal from the NFGN platform
Suspension of account privileges
Permanent account termination
Loss of commissions and earnings
Reporting to applicable authorities
Civil legal action where permitted by law
NFGN reserves the right to investigate any submitted information and verify business legitimacy before granting approval.

Create the following provider approval statuses:
Draft
Pending Submission
Pending Review
Additional Information Requested
Approved
Rejected

Only providers with an "Approved" status may publish Pay As You Go services.
Admin Approval Area
Create a new admin section:
Admin Dashboard → NFGN → Pay As You Go Provider Approvals
Admin should be able to:
Review provider applications
View uploaded documents
View uploaded location photos
Verify Pro Membership status
Approve providers
Reject providers
Request additional information
Suspend providers
Add internal notes
Track approval dates
Service Publishing Restrictions
Prevent any Pay As You Go service from becoming publicly visible until:
✓ Provider is a Pro Member
✓ Provider verification is complete
✓ Provider status is Approved
✓ Required documents have been submitted
✓ Required photos have been submitted
✓ NFGN approval has been granted
Only after all requirements have been met should the system activate the provider's Pay As You Go listings.
Final Requirement
Create a professional, mobile-responsive onboarding and verification process that protects customers, protects NFGN, and ensures that only legitimate businesses are allowed to offer Pay As You Go services on the platform.
